Mutual Funds - Katta Investments

Investing in Mutual Funds with Katta Investments is a smart way to grow your wealth. Mutual funds pool money from multiple investors to create a diversified portfolio managed by financial experts. Whether you're seeking high returns through Equity Funds, stability via Debt Funds, or a balanced approach with Hybrid Funds, we have the right solution for you. For tax savings, our ELSS Funds provide the added advantage of deductions under Section 80C.

By investing in mutual funds, you enjoy professional management, reduced risk through diversification, and higher growth potential than traditional savings methods. Plus, mutual funds are flexible - easy to buy, sell, and track online.

Systematic Investment Plan - Katta Investments

Investing through a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) with Katta Investments is a smart and disciplined way to build wealth over time. SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount in mutual funds at regular intervals, helping you benefit from rupee-cost averaging and the power of compounding.

With SIP, you can start small - investing as little as ₹500 per month - making it accessible for everyone. It eliminates the need to time the market, reducing the impact of market volatility. Over time, these small investments can grow into significant wealth thanks to consistent contributions and compounding returns.

Health Insurance - Katta Investments

Protect your health and secure your future with Health Insurance from Katta Investments. Health insurance covers medical expenses such as hospitalization, surgeries, and preventive care, ensuring you and your family are financially protected during medical emergencies.

With options like Individual Plans for personal coverage, Family Floater Plans for your entire household, and Critical Illness Insurance for severe conditions like cancer or heart disease, we offer plans tailored to your needs. For elderly individuals, our Senior Citizen Plans provide comprehensive coverage with added benefits.

Term Insurance - Katta Investments

Ensure your family's financial security with Term Insurance from Katta Investments. Term insurance is a pure protection plan that offers a high life cover at an affordable premium, providing crucial support to your loved ones in case of an unfortunate event.

With term insurance, your family receives a substantial payout that can help manage daily expenses, debts, and future financial goals. It also offers tax benefits under Section 80C, making it a smart investment for both protection and savings.

Demat Account - Katta Investments

Simplify your stock market investments with a Demat Account from Katta Investments. A Demat (Dematerialized) Account securely holds your shares, bonds, and other securities in digital form, making trading safer, faster, and more convenient.

With a Demat account, you enjoy secure and paperless transactions, reducing the risk of theft, forgery, or document loss. It allows you to easily buy, sell, and manage your investments online, ensuring seamless participation in the stock market. Additionally, a Demat account is essential for investing in IPOs and offers a hassle-free way to track your portfolio.
